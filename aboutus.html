D’s and D’s stands for Deals and Discounts. The D’s and D’s is an online website which provides all information regarding the deals and discounts prevailing in the shopping stores. The user can access the details of the deals and discounts in a shopping store. The users can read the details and know about the current offers in a shopping store they are interested in. They can keep a track of all the offers and even segregate them on the basis of their need. The website provides information regarding the offers in a shopping store. It provides information like what kind of offers are prevailing, for instance there may be a clearance sale or a 30% discount on clothing. The website even provides information on what kind of clothing the offers are applicable, for instance the offers may be applicable on women’s clothing or men’s clothing particularly. Sometimes there are offers that are applicable to only a particular type of clothing. The details regarding this kind of offers are also available in our website. When the user visits the website, the home page is displayed. The user can just give a glance at the basic details of the website. The user can get an idea on the description of the website. Then they can figure out how they can use the website. They can utilize the website according to their need. They can visit the store in which the offers seem interesting and really worth to them. The user can select their city from the a dropdown menu. The website tracks all the offers available in the nearby location. The offers are displayed to the user. If the user likes any offer, they can visit the offer and read the information in detail. As per the details they can decide on which offer they can shop in the stores.
